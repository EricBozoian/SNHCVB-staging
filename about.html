About

Welcome to Southern NH Competitive Volleyball - the premier NH-based group for competitive volleyball players! Whether you're into indoor, grass, or sand volleyball, we've got you covered with our exciting events. We take pride in being a welcoming, friendly, and highly competitive group of players. At our events, we focus on different levels of play and skill requirements, and our hosts are dedicated to creating balanced and enjoyable games by carefully assembling teams based on position knowledge and skill. To maintain the quality of our community, we carefully screen potential members based on attendance, skill, and attitude, so please make sure you meet the skill requirements for the level you want to play at. Join our growing volleyball community and experience the fun and excitement for yourself! And if you're interested in hosting an event or have suggestions for a new playing location, please don't hesitate to message us.
