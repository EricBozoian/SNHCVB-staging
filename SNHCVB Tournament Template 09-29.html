Yankee Disclaimer:

This tournament is independently organized and is not a Yankee Volleyball Tournament. This event is run in a similar format for the enjoyment of the volleyball community, but it is entirely separate from Yankee Volleyball.

General Rules:

TThe tournament will follow the official rules of USA Volleyball (USAV). All players are required to wear shirts with numbers clearly displayed on both the front and back. Both men and women are allowed to block and spike in the front row. Participants must be at least 18 years old. New players will be assessed by the tournament director to determine their skill level. Each match will be played to 21 points, with each round consisting of two games. The net will be set to {{netheight}} height standards.

Team Composition:

  • Teams must have a minimum of six players, including a designated Team Captain.
  • Teams can include any gender combination.
  • Teams can have a maximum of 9 players.
  • Minimum 2 ladies on the court at all times.

Prizes:

1st place prize: choice of backpack or hoodie per player, plus a trophy for the team.
2nd place: choice of water bottle, drawstring bag, or t-shirts per player, plus a trophy for the team.
3rd place: choice of sunglasses, stickers, or volleyball jibbitz per player.
After-party at Margaritas Nashua for all teams!

SNHCVB Rating Scale:

  • Expert (X): 7 points (equivalent to a Yankee B
  • Advanced Plus (AP): 6 points (equivalent to a Yankee B)
  • Advanced (A): 5 points (equivalent to a Yankee B-)
  • Intermediate Plus (IP): 4 points (equivalent to a Yankee C+)
  • Intermediate (I): 3 points (equivalent to a Yankee C
  • Novice Plus (NP): 2 points (equivalent to a Yankee C-)

Top three teams enter playoffs, the top team gets a bye in the first round and plays the winner of the first playoff round.

Cancellation Policy:

You may cancel up to 21 days before the tournament for a full refund. You will get a full refund after 21 days only if the tournament is full and your team is replaced.

Weather Cancellation Policy:

Tournament Cancellation due to weather or other unforeseen circumstances will be decided by 6:30 a.m. on the day of the tournament. All captains will be emailed at whatever contact information they provide. If captains do not have weekend e-mail access, please let Eric know in advance.
